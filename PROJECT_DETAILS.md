## 1. Classlly
**Type:** Cross Platform App  
**Status:** In Development  
**Role:** Founder & Product Architect  
**Duration:** Ongoing  
**Tech Stack:** Flutter, Dart, Cross-Platform  

**Description:**  
An academic manager and note-taking cross-platform app for students.

**The Journey (Story):**  
Started as a personal problem while working and studying. I needed a way to organize my academic life but found existing solutions too complex or fragmented. I built Classlly with zero prior coding skills, leveraging AI tools like ChatGPT and DeepSeek to bridge the gap between my ideas and execution.

**Challenges & Solutions:**  
The primary challenge wasn't the code—which AI helped me navigate—but the architecture of the solution itself. I had to conceptually map out how to unify notes, tasks, and schedules into a single, intuitive flow that didn't feel overwhelming for a student.

**What's Next:**  
I plan to integrate a more advanced AI tutor, refine the note-taking experience with Apple Pencil support, and launch a beta version for testing at my university.

**Key Learnings:**  
Conceptual System Design, AI Orchestration, Product Architecture, Problem-Solution Mapping.

## 2. <DevD>
**Type:** B2B SaaS / Agency Tool  
**Status:** In Development  
**Role:** Lead Architect & AI Orchestrator  
**Duration:** Ongoing (Started 2026)  
**Tech Stack:** React 19, Firebase, Gemini AI, Tailwind CSS  

**Description:**  
A comprehensive AI-driven Sales & Project Management ecosystem for software agencies.

**The Journey (Story):**  
<DevD> was built to bridge the gap between sales and development in a small freelance agency. It’s not just a CRM; it’s an end-to-end engine that takes a project from a cold lead, conducts an AI-led discovery interview, generates a technical proposal, and finally tracks the development tasks in a unified dashboard.

**Challenges & Solutions:**  
The core conceptual challenge was the "Role-Based Experience." I had to design a logic that seamlessly shifts the interface between a Sales-focused view (revenue, leads) and a Dev-focused view (deadlines, repo links) without fragmenting the data or the user flow.

**What's Next:**  
I aim to implement automated client reporting and a more advanced AI prototype generator that produces multi-page React components instead of single-file HTML.

**Key Learnings:**  
Complex System Orchestration, Role-Based Access Control Design, Real-time Data Syncing (Firebase), B2B Workflow Automation.

## 3. Sesiune
**Type:** iOS App (Native)  
**Status:** Active  
**Role:** Product Architect & iOS Dev  
**Duration:** 2025 - 2026  
**Tech Stack:** Swift, SwiftUI, Gemini API, Vision Framework  

**Description:**  
A specialized academic manager tailored specifically for FSEGA students at UBB Cluj-Napoca.

**The Journey (Story):**  
While Classlly aims for a broad audience, Sesiune was born from a "niche-down" strategy. I wanted to create a tool that speaks the specific language of FSEGA—handling our unique grading systems and schedules. It features a custom AI Subject Importer that uses Vision and Gemini to instantly extract subjects directly from screenshots of the faculty's schedule.

**Challenges & Solutions:**  
The core challenge was the conceptual design of the "AI Subject Importer." I had to design a logic flow that could accurately map visual data from schedule screenshots into a structured app format without requiring manual entry from the student.

**What's Next:**  
I aim to launch and promote Sesiune across the FSEGA community at UBB, targeting mass adoption among students who need a more tailored academic management tool.

**Key Learnings:**  
Niche Product Strategy, OCR & AI Data Mapping, Native iOS UX, Specialized Information Architecture.

**Role:** iOS Developer  
**Duration:** 2 Months  
**Tech Stack:** SwiftUI, CoreHaptics  

**Description:**  
Nostalgic music player recreating the click-wheel experience.

**The Journey (Story):**

Getting the haptic feedback to perfectly match the rotational gesture was technically demanding. It required precise calculation of touch coordinates and fine-tuning the haptic engine parameters.

**Key Learnings:**
